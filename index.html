About Me

Reneé is a virtual assistant and freelance designer from Nueva Ecija, bringing a background in the food industry. She transitioned to explore new opportunities to leverage her expertise and pursue her passion. Committed to continuous improvement, Renee independently acquired essential skills, viewing each task as an opportunity to learn.

Reneé started as a Quality Control Inspector in the food industry, where she ensures adherence to high standards. Transitioning into virtual assistance, Reneé provided personalized support and maintaining brand consistency through graphic creation and website optimization.

As a freelance Graphic and Web Designer, she not only crafts solutions for clients but also shares her expertise by tutoring students in design principles and coding languages. Her varied experiences underscore her adaptability, organizational skills, and attention to detail across multiple roles.
