Branding

For this project, I created a brand identity for a gaming content creator. I created social media posts, a business card, streaming elements, and stream overlays.

My main inspiration for this project was the retro arcade video games I used to love as a kid. The business card was both the most fun and most stressful part of this project. Since I wanted the whole brand to be inspired by retro video games, I made the business card designed like it was a Gameboy game cartridge. Everything was made from scratch, including the PCB! The stream overlays are also inspired by layout of different retro games.

I used Adobe Photoshop and Aseprite for this project.
