Product Design

Lazy Susan is a product I created as a final project for my UI/UX specialist class. As someone indecisive yet fond of food, I designed this mobile app that helps you decide which restaurant to eat at, based on distance, preferences, and restrictions--if you have any. It reduces cognitive load by narrowing down choices for you.

It asks the question "Do you want to eat here?" instead of the hard-to-answer question "Where do you want to eat?" Plus, it uses the swiping interface that makes deciding where to eat a seamless experience!

I also worked as a virtual assistant at the time I designed this where one of my responsibilities included finding restaurant options. This app could help not just me but also the ones I assist in my job. I made this using Figma.
